classic arcade game

(1) Choose one of the five avatars below by clicking on it
(2) Use your keyboard arrow keys to move your avatar around
(3) Your aim is to cross the busy road to reach the water's edge and get 10 points
(4) Be careful - if a bug hits you then you'll lose a life, a point & have to restart
(5) The bugs move at random speeds which are reset after every collision & success
Good Luck!
